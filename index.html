Social Programming

If all you want is a tutoring service, we offer that too. But what makes SummerTech unique – as we’ve heard from nearly 20 years of satisfied campers and parents – is our belief in the importance of community and social growth. Our counselors love to pack every night with social events, whether they be things they loved as kids or original experiments for the next generation. A regular evening with us might include:

  • Group board games and collaborative online puzzles and brainteasers
  • Competitive and cooperative gaming tournaments
  • Talent shows, sketches, and original comedy
  • Social games of leadership and deduction, like Mafia
  • Opportunities to share beloved fanbases through watch parties, album parties, and Cartoon Night
  • Classic camp mainstays like Tie-Dye, Arts And/Or Crafts, and Let’s All Throw Zach In The Lake*

Your Daily SummerTech Virtual Schedule

What can you expect from a day at SummerTech? Check out our daily summer camp schedule for Monday through Thursday:

9–10 am: 1st Class – Campers attend their first class of the day in their chosen course.
10-11 am: Game/Social Break – Dive into your portal to discover morning activity options.
11 am–12 pm: 2nd Class – Campers attend their second class of the day in their chosen course.
12:00 pm: Break for lunch!
12:30 pm: Electives – Anything and everything. These are counselor envisioned and counselor-camper executed creative, sporty and sometimes wacky activities where everyone has fun. Counselors create new ones each week, so we enjoy whatever young minds come up with. Examples include everything from ‘Introduction to Code’ to ‘Improvisational Comedy.’
2-3 pm: Wellness/Social Activity – Community-based activities with a wide range of choices. Choose something different every day!
6:30 pm: Evening Social Activities/Gaming – Your portal is now full of choices and options around every corner. Gaming tournaments, art events, music events, social gatherings of all kinds. Our famous SummerTech evenings done virtually but done right!
